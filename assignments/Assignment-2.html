Flowchart & Algorithm

Coffee Making

  • Step 1: Check whether the coffee should be hot or cold.
  • Step 2: If cold, prepare cold water/milk and add coffee concentrate; else if hot, boil the water and add coffee powder, allow it to brew/steep.
  • Step 3: Add milk/sugar if needed.
  • Step 4: Pour the coffee into a cup and serve it.
  • Step 5: Stop the process.

Episode 1: The Prison Break

  • Step 1: Insert the key into the dial.
  • Step 2: Check if the dial’s color is green, go to step 4. Else go to step 3.
  • Step 3: Get the key to the next position and check the dial’s color again.
  • Step 4: Unlock the door and escape the prison cell.
  • Step 5: Stop the process.

Episode 2: The Resistance

  • Step 1: Check for a person with green eyes.
  • Step 2: If found,
    • a) Check whether they wear glasses and their name contains 2 vowels.
    • b) If no glasses, check whether their name has three vowels.
    • c) If neither, check if they have red hair and their name has at least two consecutive letters.
  • Step 3: If step two fails, go to step 1. Else go to step 4.
  • Step 4: Leader found, initiate contact.
  • Step 5: Stop the process.

Episode 3: The Furnace Bots

  • Step 1: Pick a bot and check its furnace number.
  • Step 2: If the furnace number is zero, go to step 4. Else go to step 3.
  • Step 3: Check for the bot with its serial number matching the recent bot’s furnace number. Repeat from step 2.
  • Step 4: Insert the hijack code into the bot with furnace number 0.
  • Step 5: Stop the process.

Episode 4: The Train Heist

  • Step 1: Assign a variable with value zero as the starting position.
  • Step 2:
    • a) If the train moves left, decrement the variable by 1.
    • b) If it moves right, increment the variable by 1.
  • Step 3: Check if the variable reached 10.
    • a) If yes, go to step 4.
    • b) If not, go to step 2.
  • Step 4: Steal the ‘node of power’.
  • Step 5: Stop the process.
